Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how our AI marketing engine works? We've compiled answers to the most common questions from North Reno business owners.

How quickly will I start seeing results?

Most of our clients see their first qualified leads within 48 hours of going live. However, the real magic happens over the first 2-4 weeks as the AI learns about your business, your customers, and optimizes its approach. By week 4, most clients report a 3-5x increase in qualified lead volume compared to their previous methods. The exact timeline depends on your current traffic volume and conversion baseline, but we've found that patience combined with our optimization typically yields remarkable results within the first month.

Will the AI chatbot actually handle customer service, or just collect leads?

Our AI is incredibly versatile. It can handle both lead generation and customer service simultaneously. Many of our clients use it to answer common questions, schedule appointments, process refunds, handle complaints, and much more. The AI can be customized to handle whatever tasks make sense for your business. Some clients use it primarily for lead capture, while others use it for comprehensive customer support. You have complete control over what conversations the AI handles and when it escalates to your human team. This flexibility is one of the biggest advantages of our platform.

What if the AI gives wrong information to a customer?

This is a great question and something we take very seriously. Our AI is trained on your specific business information, FAQs, and knowledge base, so it has accurate information to reference. We also build in multiple safeguards: the AI can be configured to escalate complex questions to humans, it provides sources for its answers, and it's designed to say "I don't know" rather than make up information. Additionally, all conversations are logged and reviewed, so you can monitor quality and make corrections. We also recommend regularly updating your knowledge base as your business evolves. Most clients find that the AI is more consistent and accurate than their human team after the initial training period.

How much does this cost compared to hiring a customer service team?

The cost comparison is dramatic. A single full-time customer service representative costs $30,000-$50,000+ per year in salary, benefits, and overhead. Our platform costs a fraction of that and can handle the work of 3-5 employees. Most small to mid-size businesses see ROI within the first 3-6 months. Plus, you get consistency, 24/7 availability, and the ability to scale instantly without hiring and training new staff. When you factor in reduced customer churn from faster response times and improved lead quality, the financial case for AI becomes overwhelming. We're happy to run a custom ROI analysis for your specific business during your free consultation.

What happens if your service goes down? Will I lose leads?

We maintain 99.9% uptime with redundant servers across multiple data centers. In the unlikely event of service disruption, we have automatic failover systems to keep your AI running. Additionally, all lead data is backed up in real-time to secure cloud storage, so you'll never lose customer information. We also provide fallback email capture forms that activate if there's any issue with the chatbot, ensuring you never miss a lead. Our infrastructure is enterprise-grade, and we invest heavily in reliability because we understand that your business depends on our service. We also include 24/7 technical support to address any issues immediately.
